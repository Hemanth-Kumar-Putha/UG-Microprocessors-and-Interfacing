# Microprocessors and Interfacing Teaching Assistanship

This branch is for all my work as a student and TA for the 4 credit Compulsory Disciplinary Course (CDC) for all the circuital branches (CSE, ECE, EEE, ENI).

I completed this course in the second semester of my sophomore year (2nd year) after I returned from the Intra-campus transfer (to BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus) for recovering from my medical issues. In the next year, i.e. second semester of my pre-final year (3rd year), I became the TA for this course. 

In this TAship, I was tasked with helping the students understand the ASM language (x86 assembly language) in the labs. I was assigned two sections (combined strength of ~80 students) and each section has 2 TAs. 

In this TAship, I helped students clarify their doubts in the weekly labs, debugged the issues they were facing in the weekly labs and guided students in writing optimal 8086 assembly program.
